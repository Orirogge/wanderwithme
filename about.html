ABOUT

My name is Ori Roggemans. I'm a 19-year old guy studying graphical and digital media with a passion for photo- and videography. Together with my beautful friends and even more beautiful girlfriend I visit and travel to places we've never seen before. I try to capture every wonderful moment with my camera and shoot amazing pictures and videos.

For my photos and videos I use the Nikon D3300 with the Kit lens 18-55mm. For sound I use the Rode VideoMicro attached on my Nikon. I also own a cheap tripod for some nice selfies and timed shots. For some quick pictures I shoot with my Motorola Moto G3.
